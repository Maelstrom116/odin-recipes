Mashed Potatoes!

Description

These mashed potatoes are a family recipe that are quite literally the best you'll have tasted. Forget anything you new about potatoes, that's the past and you've arrived at greatness.

Whenever I bring these potatoes, they're the first thing that's finished and I bring A LOT. Every event, party, gathering, whatever, I'm asked to bring these masterful spuds along. Good luck and god speed. You'll be invited to move events than you ever cared to attend!

  • 3 pounds of russet potatoes
  • 5 tablespoons of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of pepper
  • 1-1.5 sticks of salted butter
  • 3 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • Horseradish, not cream preferably, to taste
  1. Skin the potatoes
  2. Cut the skinned potatoes into 1-1.5 inch cubes
  3. Place cut potatoes into a pot of water; bring pot to a boil
  4. Upon boiling, reduce to a simmer and cook until the potatoes slide off a fork
  5. Drain potatoes in colander for 5 minutes
  6. Return potatoes to the empty pot, mash up the potatoes. I use the handblender without turning it on
  7. Add the salt, pepper, butter, half the heavy whipping cream, and a teaspoon of horseradish
  8. Mix with a hand blender
  9. Add remaining whipping cream and more of any ingredient if desired
  10. THAT'S IT!
