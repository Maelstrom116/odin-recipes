Grilled Steak!

Odin Recipe's

I don't know what it is, but the steaks from Costco, and the right seasoning are just perfection. Nothing tastes better than this when you're in the mood for a good steak.

I've been cooking this one for about 8 or 10 years. I've never deviated because there is no reason that anyone could say that would make me change my mind. This is the best. Steak houses don't hold a candle to this, just you wait.

  • Costco Sirloin Steak
  • McCormicks Montreal Steak Seasoning
  1. Heat the grill
  2. Season steak, preferably 24 hours
  3. Have steaks at room temperature, usually 20 minutes out of fridge
  4. Cook the steaks to your desired temperature. Anything over Medium is the devil's work.
  5. ENJOY!
